Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct v2.1

The short version

Our project strives to be a welcoming and respectful community for everyone. We ask that all participants in MCP Ambassador projects abide by the following code of conduct.

The long version

1. Purpose

This document outlines expectations for participation in MCP Ambassador projects, provides steps for reporting unacceptable behavior, and describes how reports are handled.

2. Scope

This code of conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

3. Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission

4. Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards of acceptable behavior. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may be removed from their roles.

5. Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting: security@mcpambassador.dev

6. Enforcement

Reports will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. Possible responses include a private warning, temporary or permanent ban from project spaces, or referral to legal authorities.
